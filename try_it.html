How to use it

Using the silicon data scientist is extremely simple. We've abstracted away all the complexity so anyone can use it, no matter their technical expertise.

Prepare an Email

Simply write an email describe the work that needs to be done. This can be anything from data cleansing to financial forecasting to a full Kaggle Contest. Our agentic network will sweat the details.

Attach your Data

Simply attach your raw data as a .CSV or an Excel file. Our agentic network will manage all the files you've sent. And don't sweat the size or context window, even if you send a huge google drive link, our Silicon Data Scientist can handle files up to 10GB.

A Rapid Response

Our Silicon Data scientist will autonomously take a look at your data, design an architecture to answer your request, execute against that plan, automatically tackle any bugs that pop up, and send you an email with your completed request when it's finished. Couldn't be simpler!
